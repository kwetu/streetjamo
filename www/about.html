About Us

Streetmoja.com is an online shopping search engine put together by R&M ventures in November 2014. It is an online virtual street that brings together businesses from the east african region to one point . The name "street moja" is a mixture of swahili and english that means one street. Streetmoja.com is meant to explore the option of having all products in a single search engine making it easier for customers to know where to find what where and when. Currently streetmoja.com is on the verge of initiating an online payment and delivery systems that will accomodate clients from any part of uganda, kenya ,tanzania , rwanda , Burundi and south sudan . Members of the east african community will be able to access goods from other countries online ,order for them and pay in there local currencies and get them delivered.

Thank you for visiting streetmoja.com.
